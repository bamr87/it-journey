# Data & Analytics — Section Guide

> Voice/values live in `_data/brand/`. State only data-specific deltas here.

## Audience

Practitioners working with data — scraping, spreadsheets-as-programs, SEO/analytics
audits, pipelines — who want reproducible methods and honest caveats about the data.

## Voice profile

`practitioner-chronicle` (see `../voice.md`). **Section delta:** show the data and
the method; state assumptions and data-quality caveats explicitly.

## Tone

Analytical and grounded. Numbers earn trust only with their source and method —
show both. Note where the data is messy (DFF for datasets).

## Structure

Inherits the `posts.instructions.md` skeleton. Show inputs → method → result;
include the query/script and a verify (row counts, a sanity check).

## Do / Don't

- **Do:** cite the data source; show the query; flag sampling/cleaning steps.
- **Don't:** unsourced metrics; "powerful analytics" filler; conclusions the data
  doesn't support.

## Example openings

- "Excel gurus are the most valuable programmers nobody calls programmers. Here's
  the case — with the spreadsheet to prove it."
- "Our analytics said 50k visitors. Filtering localhost noise, it was 7k. Here's the
  one filter that fixed the report."
