# Technology — Section Guide

> Voice/values live in `_data/brand/`. State only technology-specific deltas here.

## Audience

Practitioners and learners after the bigger picture — how technologies fit together,
mental models, and durable principles rather than a single how-to.

## Voice profile

`concept-essay` (see `../voice.md`). **Section delta:** synthesize and explain; a
clear mental model the reader keeps is the goal.

## Tone

Explanatory and durable. Favor principles that outlast the tool-of-the-month;
illustrate with concrete examples from real work.

## Structure

Inherits the `posts.instructions.md` skeleton, concept-led: idea → why it matters →
example → how to apply. Diagrams (Mermaid) for relationships.

## Do / Don't

- **Do:** explain the "why" and the mental model; ground abstractions in a concrete
  case; link the deeper how-to.
- **Don't:** trend-chasing; "cutting-edge"/"revolutionary" filler; abstraction with
  no example.

## Example openings

- "Build, destroy, repeat — the loop that turns a fragile setup into one you
  actually understand."
- "Most 'new' technology is an old idea with better defaults. Knowing which idea
  saves you a lot of learning."
