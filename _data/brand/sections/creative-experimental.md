# Creative & Experimental — Section Guide

> Voice/values live in `_data/brand/`. State only creative-specific deltas here.

## Audience

Learners and builders following playful experiments — AI-generated stories, fun
side projects, "what if I tried…" pieces. The sandbox of the site.

## Voice profile

`concept-essay` (see `../voice.md`), loosened. **Section delta:** this is the most
playful section — voice can be whimsical and narrative, as long as it still says
something. Experimental framing is encouraged here (this section relaxes some
"discouraged" terms in `.cms/config.yml › brand`).

## Tone

Playful, curious, generous. Experiments are allowed to be half-baked — say what you
tried, what surprised you, and what you'd do next (REnO/MVP in spirit).

## Structure

Looser than the standard skeleton. Keep one H1; still include language tags on code
and a "what I'd try next". A through-line beats a random pile of ideas.

## Do / Don't

- **Do:** show the experiment and the surprising bits; be honest when it flopped
  (DFF); have fun.
- **Don't:** mistake whimsy for substance-free; skip the takeaway; bury the one
  interesting result.

## Example openings

- "I asked an AI to write a children's story about a unicorn baker. What came back
  taught me more about prompting than any tutorial."
- "This started as a joke project. Three commits later it was teaching me something
  real about state machines."
