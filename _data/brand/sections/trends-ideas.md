# Trends & Ideas — Section Guide

> Voice/values live in `_data/brand/`. State only trends-specific deltas here.

## Audience

AI-enthusiasts and forward-looking practitioners thinking about where things are
going — speculation grounded in evidence, not hype.

## Voice profile

`concept-essay` (see `../voice.md`). **Section delta:** opinions welcome, but earn
them; separate "what I think" from "what's known".

## Tone

Curious and skeptical. Excitement is fine; unfounded hype is not. A good trends
piece ages well because it reasoned, not just predicted.

## Structure

Inherits the `posts.instructions.md` skeleton, argument-led: observation → reasoning
→ implication → what to watch. Cite the evidence behind the trend.

## Do / Don't

- **Do:** distinguish evidence from speculation; give the reader something to do or
  watch for; cite sources.
- **Don't:** breathless futurism; "game-changing" filler; predictions with no
  reasoning. (This section relaxes `cutting-edge` in `.cms/config.yml › brand`, but
  use it sparingly.)

## Example openings

- "Everyone's predicting an AI takeover of programming. The more interesting question
  is which part it *won't* touch — and why."
- "An impossible triangle is a useful model for software trade-offs. Here's the one
  most roadmaps pretend doesn't exist."
