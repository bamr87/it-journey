# Learning — Section Guide

> Voice/values live in `_data/brand/`. State only learning-specific deltas here.

## Audience

Learners and educators — the heart of the zer0 → her0 mission. Posts about *how to
learn IT*, learning-path design, and the project's own pedagogy (quests, bashcrawl).

## Voice profile

`practitioner-chronicle` (see `../voice.md`). **Section delta:** make the learning
objective explicit; model the encouraging, non-gatekeeping voice the whole brand
aspires to.

## Tone

Warm, patient, and motivating. This is where "meet the reader where they are" matters
most. Celebrate small wins; normalize being a `n00b`.

## Structure

Inherits the `posts.instructions.md` skeleton. State learning objectives up front;
map takeaways back to them at the end; link the relevant quest/path.

## Do / Don't

- **Do:** name the objective and the audience level; connect theory to a concrete
  exercise; credit the methods/sources you build on (COLAB).
- **Don't:** gatekeep ("obviously you just…"); "comprehensive guide" filler; theory
  with no path to practice.

## Example openings

- "Teaching the terminal as a dungeon crawl sounds gimmicky — until you watch a
  beginner actually stick with it. Here's why it works."
- "Everyone says 'learn by building'. Here's the smallest project that actually
  teaches the most."
