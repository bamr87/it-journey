# Culture & Society — Section Guide

> Voice/values live in `_data/brand/`. State only culture-specific deltas here.

## Audience

Educators and reflective practitioners interested in how technology meets people —
the social context the rest of the site's how-to work sits inside.

## Voice profile

`concept-essay` (see `../voice.md`). **Section delta:** essays carry a clear thesis
and respect the reader's intelligence; evidence and fairness matter more than heat.

## Tone

Thoughtful, fair, and specific. Strong points, handled with care. This section
touches the brand's mission directly — equalizing access, empowering people.

## Structure

Inherits the `posts.instructions.md` skeleton, argument-led: thesis → evidence →
nuance → so-what. Cite sources for factual and historical claims.

## Do / Don't

- **Do:** ground claims in sources; acknowledge counterpoints; connect to the
  mission of broadening access.
- **Don't:** hot-take-as-fact; sweeping generalizations; treating a contested topic
  as settled.

## Example openings

- "The injustice we build into systems is rarely malicious — it's defaulted in.
  Here's where that starts."
- "Every information revolution widens a gap before it closes one. This one is no
  different — and that's the part we can change."
