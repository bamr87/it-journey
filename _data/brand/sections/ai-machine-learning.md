# AI & Machine Learning — Section Guide

> Voice/values live in `_data/brand/`. State only AI/ML-specific deltas here.

## Audience

Practitioners and AI-augmented builders who already use AI tools and want real
patterns — agentic workflows, Copilot/MCP in practice — with the human-review loop
intact.

## Voice profile

`practitioner-chronicle` (see `../voice.md`). **Section delta:** AIPD is the
governing value — be transparent about what AI did, and honest about what it got
right *and wrong*.

## Tone

Curious and rigorous, never breathless. Treat AI as a collaborator, not magic. Show
the prompt, the output, and the human judgment applied to it.

## Structure

Inherits the `posts.instructions.md` skeleton. For AI-session chronicles, include
the session metadata + prompt highlights + "what the AI got right/wrong" blocks
from `posts.instructions.md` §4.

## Do / Don't

- **Do:** show real prompts and real (sometimes wrong) outputs; name the model and
  date; cite sources for claims about capabilities.
- **Don't:** imply blind trust in AI; use "revolutionary"/"game-changing"; present
  a model's output as fact without review.

## Example openings

- "I asked the model to refactor a 600-line script. It got 80% right — and the
  20% it missed is exactly where this post lives."
- "MCP servers sound abstract until you wire one to your repo. Here's the one that
  finally made it click."
