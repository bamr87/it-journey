How Progression Works

The journey spans 16 binary-coded levels (0000–1111) across four tiers, each worth more XP than the last. Every quest is rated by one of four canonical difficulties:

🟢 Easy 🟡 Medium 🔴 Hard ⚔️ Epic

The full taxonomy is generated from scripts/quest/quest_registry.py (the single source of truth), so it never drifts out of sync.
